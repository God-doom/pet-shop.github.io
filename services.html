Welcome to Jack's services for you

At our pet shop, we’re dedicated to providing everything your furry, feathered, and finned friends need to stay happy and healthy. From high-quality pet food and fun toys to grooming, accessories, and expert care advice, we offer a wide range of products and services designed to support pets of all kinds. Whether you’re looking to spoil your companion with treats or need essentials for their everyday comfort, our team is here to help you find exactly what you need. Your pet’s well‑being is our top priority!

pet daycare

Our pet daycare offers a safe, fun, and stimulating environment where your furry friends can play, socialise, and relax while you’re away. With caring staff, plenty of toys, secure play areas, and regular activity sessions, we make sure every pet enjoys their day to the fullest. Whether your pet loves energetic playtime or prefers quiet cuddles, we tailor the experience to their personality. At our daycare, your pet’s happiness, comfort, and well‑being always come first.

Pet Photography

Capture your pet’s personality with our professional Pet Photography service! Whether they’re playful, curious, or full of attitude, we specialise in creating beautiful, high‑quality photos that truly showcase your pet’s character. With a calm, pet‑friendly setup and plenty of patience, we make the experience fun and stress‑free for both pets and owners. From stunning portraits to action shots, we turn special moments into memories you can cherish forever. Perfect for birthdays, milestones, or just because your pet deserves the spotlight!

Pet Training Classes

Our Pet Training Classes are designed to help your furry companions learn good manners, build confidence, and develop strong bonds with their owners. With patient, positive, and reward‑based methods, we cover everything from basic obedience and socialisation to more advanced skills. Whether you have a playful puppy or an adult pet needing a refresher, our experienced trainers create a fun, supportive environment where every pet can succeed. We’re here to make training enjoyable, effective, and tailored to your pet’s unique personality!
